What it does

Every feature a
musician needs.

MEtronome was built by someone who has sat in practice rooms for years. Nothing you don't need. Everything you do.

⏱
Precision Engine

AVAudioEngine with hardware-level DispatchSourceTimer. No web-based clock drift. Real timing at any BPM.

👆
Tap Tempo

Find any tempo instantly. Tap the beat and the engine locks in — then practice from there.

🎵
Time Signatures

2/4 through 7/8 and beyond. Accent patterns, subdivisions, mixed meter — it's all here.

📊
Practice Tracker

Log every session. See your consistency over time. Know which tempos you've actually practiced.

🎯
Goals System

Set a target BPM. Track your progress week over week. Watch the gap close.

📱
Bluetooth Sync

MultipeerConnectivity keeps your whole ensemble in tempo. Rehearse together, stay locked in.

📋
Playlist Mode

Build warm-up sequences. Chain tempos together for structured practice without stopping to adjust.

🔒
Lock Screen Widget

Start, stop, and adjust BPM without unlocking. The metronome is always one gesture away. (Pro)
